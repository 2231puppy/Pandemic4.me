Pandemic4.me

Welcome to Pandemic4.me! Here, your documentaries of your pandemic experience will be shown to the world!

The school and community closures during the COVID-19 worldwide pandemic are truly a part of history. How has your life changed? What does school look like? How do you celebrate special occasions? Did you get a Covid pet? Document some of the craziness that is your Pandemic4.me and share your perspective with the world.


Email kid-friendly videos to videos@pandemic4.me using a Dropbox or Google Drive link to send the videos. Make sure the link is set to everyone can view/shared! Note that the system is not automated (I have to input the files manually), so it will be 1-48 hours before the video is published.


Requirements:
  • Must have consent of all people in the video
  • Must be between 1-10 minutes long total
  • You can send mulitiple clips, and this is preferred as it gives the world a better idea of what a day in your life is like
  • Must be kid-friendly
  • Should include something that has become routine during the pandemic or something that has kept you occupied
  • As of now, you may send text (articles) and they will be added to the website

  • We are not responsible for any private information disclosed in your video. By uploading, you agree that it will be public and may not be able to be removed before it has been seen. Children under 13 should have consent of a legal guardian before sending their videos.
